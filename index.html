Missile Man of India

Name : Dr.APJ Abdul Kamlam

AGE : 83 (1931-2015)

Place : Tamilnadu

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, the former President of India, is known as the 'Missile Man of India' for his contribution to developing India's missile projects, Prithvi and Agni missiles. He also played a significant role in developing the first indigenous satellite launch vehicle.
